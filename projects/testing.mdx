## What's "Would You" All About?

Would You is a Discord bot that allows you to play popular games like "Would You Rather", "Never have I ever", "What would you do" or our brand new Higher or Lower games with your friends on Discord. It's a fun and easy way to get to know each other better and to have a good time together.
